How It Works
Detection Pipeline
01

Signal Capture

fNIRS sensor captures hemodynamic brain activity across 19 channels in real-time.

02

Preprocessing

Raw signals are filtered, normalized, and segmented into 128-timestep windows.

03

CNN-LSTM

CNN extracts spatial features; LSTM analyzes temporal patterns across signal windows.

04

Classification

Model outputs probability scores for Healthy, MCI, and Alzheimer's classes.
